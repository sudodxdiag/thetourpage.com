    • National Parks Tours
      • Serengeti National Park Serengeti National Park is a World Heritage Site teeming with wildlife: over 2 million ungulates, 4000 lions, 1000 leopard, 550 cheetahs and some 500 bird species inhabit an area close to 15,000 square kilometers in size
      • Mikumi National Park The park is rich in wildlife species like elephant, buffaloes, Impala, Lion, Zebra, Wildbeest, Baboon, warthogs, Hippos, baboon and Elands which can be spotted throughout the visit to the park despite the time of the yaer.
      • Saadan National Park Being the only National Park that harbors both marine and terrestrial habitats, Saadani has plenty of marine life and large numbers of wild animals
      • Ruaha National Park The park has a large number of biggest elephants and all other big four animals like Lions, leopards and buffalos
      • Lake Manyara National Park The Park has more than 350 species of birds and it is known for having the tree-climbing lions
      • Tarangire National Park The park is popular and famous for its large elephants found along the life-line mainly River Tarangire which flows during the dry season
      Mountain Climbing Tours
      • Mount Kilimanjaro It is the highest mountain in Africa and the highest single free-standing mountain above sea level in the world, having 5,895 m (19,341 ft) above sea level
      • Mount Meru It is the second-highest mountain in Tanzania, after Mount Kilimanjaro. Mount Meru is also the highest mountain/point in Arusha Region
      • Udzungwa Mountains Udzungwa Mountains rise up steeply from the western edge of the Selous Game Reserve. Vervet monkeys play high in in the forest canopy, and small forest antelopes can be viewed at the right time of the day.
      • Usambara Mountains these mountains offer a captivating landscape with their western and eastern ranges divided by a picturesque valley dotted with small villages and farms
